Inclusion criteria

  • willing/able to swallow 9–12 capsules per day with food and attend all study appointments to complete questionnaires
  • meet criteria for ADHD as assessed by the CASI-5, with symptoms present in more than one setting and causing impairment in social or academic functioning
  • have at least one symptom of irritability or anger from the ODD or DMDD subscale questions

  • having any known abnormality of mineral metabolism (e.g., Wilson’s disease, hemochromatosis)
  • currently taking any other medication with primarily central nervous system activity, including stimulants, or having taken any within two weeks prior to enrollment
  • having severe anxiety preventing separation from parent to answer study questionnaires
  • having any disability that would interfere with participant answering questions verbally
  • not able to communicate in English
  • being pregnant or sexually active at baseline (specific to girls who started menstruating).

Diagnostic criteria

ADHD as assessed by the CASI-5, symptom of irritability or anger from the ODD or DMDD subscale questions
